My recipe for the best cinnamon rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup of warm milk
  • 5/2 teaspoons yeast
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg york
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup of dark brown suga
  • 3/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup buttter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar,eggs
  2. Melt the butter and the mixture
  3. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  4. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewher to rise for 2 hours
